AI-Based Honeypots

Bachelor's Thesis by Your Name

About the Project

This project explores how Artificial Intelligence can enhance honeypots for better threat detection, deception, and response in cybersecurity.

Problem Statement

Traditional honeypots are static and easy to detect. AI can make them adaptive, smarter, and more effective against evolving threats.

Proposed Solution

We use machine learning models to analyze attack patterns and dynamically adjust honeypot behavior, improving realism and effectiveness.

Future Work

Integrating AI-based honeypots into large-scale SOC environments and combining them with threat intel feeds and behavioral analysis.
